The RootWork Framework

Education that grows with your child

The RootWork Framework reimagines what school can be. Rooted in research, designed for the whole child, and built around living systems that teach resilience, responsibility, and real-world skills.

🌱

Garden-Based Learning Labs

Outdoor raised-bed gardens and indoor microgreens propagation labs where students cultivate scientific inquiry, environmental stewardship, and entrepreneurial thinking.

🍳

Culinary Arts Integration

From harvest to plate — students learn nutrition, food science, and life skills through hands-on cooking programs connected to our community nutrition partnerships.

📚

STEAM Across Every Subject

Science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics woven through garden data collection, environmental monitoring, design challenges, and creative expression.

💜

Therapeutic & Whole-Child Support

ACE-responsive programming, trauma-informed practices, behavioral support, and on-campus counseling — because a child who feels safe can finally learn.

Support Services

Every child deserves access to the right support

On-campus therapeutic and instructional services eliminate the burden of off-site appointments. Many services qualify for Georgia Medicaid reimbursement, making them accessible to all families.

Inquire About Services →

Licensed Counseling

Individual and group counseling services provided by licensed professionals on campus. Trauma-informed, culturally responsive, and aligned with each student's needs.

Speech-Language Pathology

On-campus SLP services for students with communication needs. No more driving across town for appointments — services happen during the school day.

Occupational Therapy

Fine motor, sensory integration, and daily living skills support delivered by licensed occupational therapists right on the UCA campus.

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA)

Behavioral intervention services designed by Board Certified Behavior Analysts. Supporting students with emotional and behavioral disorders through evidence-based strategies.

Academic Tutoring

Targeted, certified tutoring in reading, math, and test preparation. Small-group and individual sessions tailored to each student's academic plan.

Therapeutic Horticulture

Garden-based therapeutic programming that builds emotional regulation, social skills, and self-efficacy through structured horticultural activities.
